Homemade Lasagna

Photo by Emanuel Ekström on Unsplash

Description

This recipe is for Homemade Lasagna featuring a meaty, made-from-scratch tomato sauce and a cheesy filling. It's a dish that's not as difficult to make as it might seem

Ingredients

  • ½ pound ground pork
  • ½ pound lean ground beef
  • ½ cup minced onion
  • 1 clove of crushed garlic
  • 2 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 (8 ounce) can tomato sauce
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsely (divided)
  • 1 ½ teaspoons dried basil
  • 1 ½ teaspoons salt
  • ½ teaspoon dried oregano
  • ⅛ teaspoon white sugar
  • ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 (16 ounce) package lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound small-curd cottage cheese
  • ¾ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 (16 ounce) package shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 3 large eggs

Steps

  1. Cook the meat: In a skillet, cook the ground pork and ground beef until browned. Add the onion and continue cooking until it becomes translucent. Stir in the crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, half of the parsely, garlic, basil, 1.5 teaspoons of salt, oregano, and sugar. Simmer for 30 minutes.
  2. Cook the noodles: Boil the lasagna noodles in lightly salted water until they are al dente (8 to 10 minutes). While the noodles are cooking, preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  3. Make the cheese layer: In a lrge bowel, combine the cottage cheese, Parmesan cheese, eggs, the remaining parsley, remaining salt, and pepper.
  4. Assemble the lasagna: Spread a small amount of sauce on the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish. Place two layers of noodles on top of the sauce. Then, layer with half of the cheese mixture, half of the remaining sauce, and half of the mozzarella cheese. Repeat these layers once more with the remaining Ingredients.
  5. Bake the lasagna: Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake for 30 to 40 minutes. After this, remove the foil and continue baking for another 5 to 10 minutes, or until the cheese on top is golden brown.

Let the lasagna stand for 10 minutes before cutting and serving.
